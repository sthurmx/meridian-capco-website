We are not a passive check. We bring strategic counsel, operational perspective, and the full weight of the Meridian network alongside every investment we make.

Seed and angel capital for early-stage startups
Founder evaluation, due diligence, and deal structuring
Post-investment strategic support and advisory
Portfolio construction across diversified startup verticals
Introductions to follow-on investors and strategic partners
