Common Questions

Before You Inquire.

Which platforms do you manage?
We work across all major platforms — LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), TikTok, and YouTube. We recommend the right combination based on your specific audience, goals, and resources — not on what is trending.
Do you use AI to create content?
Yes — strategically. AI-assisted content production is part of our workflow, but every piece is reviewed, refined, and approved before publication. AI accelerates production without replacing the judgment required to produce content that actually resonates.
How involved do I need to be?
As involved as you want to be. We offer fully managed services where your only job is approval — and collaborative models where you contribute ideas, stories, or raw material that we develop into finished content.
How long before I see results?
Meaningful results typically emerge between 60 and 90 days of consistent, strategic execution. Social media is a compounding asset — it rewards consistency over time, not quick bursts of activity.
Do you work with personal brands or just companies?
Both. We work with individual executives and founders building personal brands alongside their organizations, and with companies managing institutional social presence. The strategy differs — the standard does not.
What makes Meridian Signal different?
We are a strategy-first operation. We do not just create content — we build presence. Every post, every platform, every piece of content connects back to a documented strategy with defined goals. We measure what matters and adjust accordingly.
