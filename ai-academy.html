Common Questions

Before You Inquire.

Do I need a technical background?
No. Our AI Foundations and business-focused tracks are specifically designed for non-technical professionals. We start where you are, not where we assume you should be.
How long does training take?
It depends on your track and format. Individual sessions run 60–90 minutes. Workshops are half-day or full-day. Corporate programs are typically structured over four to twelve weeks.
Is training delivered in-person or virtually?
Both. We offer in-person delivery in the Atlanta, Georgia market and virtual delivery nationwide. Corporate programs can be structured for hybrid delivery.
What AI tools will I learn?
We are platform-agnostic and teach the tools most relevant to your role and industry — including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and a range of specialized AI applications.
Can you train our entire company?
Yes. Our Corporate AI Program is specifically designed for organization-wide deployments. We conduct a readiness assessment, design custom curriculum by department, and scale delivery through a structured multi-cohort rollout.
How is Meridian AI Academy different?
We are practitioners, not theorists. Every module is built around what is useful today — not what is impressive in a classroom. We measure success by what you do differently on Monday morning, not what you learned on Friday afternoon.
