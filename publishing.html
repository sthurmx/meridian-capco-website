Common Questions

Before You Inquire.

Do I need to write the book myself?
No. Our ghostwriting offering is designed for leaders who want a polished, published book in their voice — without writing it themselves. This is a standard, professional service used by executives and public figures at every level.
How long does a book project take?
A complete ghostwritten manuscript typically takes four to eight months depending on scope, complexity, and your availability for interviews and reviews. Strategy-only or content platform engagements can move faster.
Do you help with self-publishing or traditional publishing?
Both. We provide pathway analysis that helps you understand the genuine tradeoffs between traditional publishing, hybrid publishing, and self-publishing — and we support execution across all three paths.
What kind of books do you work on?
We specialize in non-fiction business books — leadership, entrepreneurship, strategy, professional development, industry-specific expertise, and memoir-adjacent works by business figures. We do not work on fiction.
What if I already have a draft?
We work with existing manuscripts at any stage — whether you need structural development, editorial refinement, or a complete rewrite. We assess what you have and recommend the most efficient path forward.
Is my project kept confidential?
Absolutely. Every engagement operates under strict confidentiality. We do not disclose client relationships, projects, or manuscript details — ever. Your name on the cover is the only attribution that matters.
